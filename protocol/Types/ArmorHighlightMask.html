ArmorHighlightMask: ushort

The ArmorHighlightMask selects which armor attributes highlighting is applied to.

0x0001

ArmorLevel

0x0002

SlashingProtection

0x0004

PiercingProtection

0x0008

BludgeoningProtection

0x0010

ColdProtection

0x0020

FireProtection

0x0040

AcidProtection

0x0080

ElectricalProtection
